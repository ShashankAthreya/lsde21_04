Similarities to the Barabasi-Albert model.

The Barabasi-Albert Model depicts a graph of Network Systems. We observe the network hubs are the nodes with highest number of edges.

We can see the similarities with the slope of the Barabasi-Albert model and the User-Transaction distribution.

This leads us to beleive that there are certain hubs within the Bitcoin graphs and analysis of these hubs are interesting.

These hubs can be the platforms which allow coin trade or they can be great influencers.

Regardless, we can assume they play a dominant role in the community as they hold the highest modularity score.

We can try to map the address of these hubs to the person/entity and see thier role in the community.
